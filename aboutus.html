About us

Travel to space is a Romanian space-travel company founded in 2000. It is headquartered in Romania/Bucharest  and has its primary operational bases in USA/Nevada, China, Russia and Romania/Bucharest space-ports. In 2025, Travel to space was the largest European space-travel agency by scheduled passengers flown in space, and it carried more inter-space passengers than any other space-company. Travel to space Group operates more than 5 ZOLTAN -BZ980 space-craft, with a single BZ-990 used as a charter space-craft, as a backup, and for pilot training. The space-line has been characterised by its rapid expansion, a result of the deregulation of the space flight industry in Europe in 2026 and the success of its business model. Travel to space’s route network serves 2 planets and the Moon. The company has often been apprised for its best working and safe conditions, low use of extra charges, good customer service, and tendency intentionally to promote charity.
